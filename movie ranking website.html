The Best Movies According to Noor fatima;

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

During her family's move to the suburbs, a sullen 10-year-old girl wanders into a world ruled by gods, witches and spirits, a world where humans are changed into beasts.
This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Ex Machina

It follows a programmer who is invited by his CEO to administer the Turing test to an intelligent humanoid robot.
Really cool sci-fi movie.

Drive

a Hollywood stunt performer (Gosling) who moonlights as a wheelman for criminals discovers that a contract has been put on him after a heist gone wrong.
Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
